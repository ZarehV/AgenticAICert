
Junior High Volleyball Lesson Plans
(6th–8th Grade)
50-Minute Lesson Plans
Source: USA Volleyball

---

Day 1
Objective: Setting and Attacking Fundamentals
Warm-up: Hot Potato
Skills: Overhand Passing/Setting, Attacking
Practice: Triangle Setting, Attacking Practice
Game: 1v1 Beach Ball Transition Scoring

---

Day 2
Objective: Approach Footwork for Attacking
Warm-up: Loser Becomes the Net
Skills: Run, Jump, and Swing Attacking
Practice: Pass, Set, Hit (Groups of 3), Back-Set Triangle
Game: 2v2 Transition Scoring

---

Day 3
Objective: Torque & Overhand Serving
Warm-up: Ball-Pyramid Relay
Skills: Torque Serve, Overhand Serve
Practice: Partner Serving
Games: Team Serving Competition, 2v2 Transition Scoring

---

Day 4
Objective: Serve Receive & Ball Control
Warm-up: Ball Challenges (Over/Under, Side-to-Side, Feet-to-Feet)
Skills: Forearm Passing, OH Pass/Serve Receive
Practice: Butterfly Drill
Game: 3v3 Transition Scoring

---

Day 5
Objective: Advanced Attacking (Tip & Roll Shot)
Warm-up: 2v2 Tennis
Skills: Tipping, Roll Shot
Practice: Tip/Roll Shot Drill
Game: 3v3 Transition Scoring

---

Day 6
Objective: Defensive Skills
Warm-up: Wall Ball
Skills: Digging, Pancake
Practice: Attack, Dig & Catch; Partner Pancake
Game: 4v4 Transition Scoring

---

Day 7
Objective: Sitting Volleyball Awareness
Warm-up: 1v1 Beach Ball Cooperative
Skills: Sitting Forearm Passing, Sitting Setting
Practice: Sit-N-Set Triangle, Triple Pepper Variation
Game: 3v3 Sitting Volleyball

---

Day 8
Objective: Blocking
Warm-up: Cat & Mouse
Skills: Blocking Technique
Practice: Blocking Drill (Pass, Set, Hit + Block)
Game: 4v4 Regular Scoring

---

Day 9
Objective: Slide Jump Serve
Warm-up: Loser Becomes the Net
Skill: Slide Jump Serve
Practice: Partner Serving & Serve Receive
Game: 4v4 Regular Scoring

---

Day 10
Objective: Competitive Play
Warm-up: Speedball
Game: 5v5 Regular Scoring

---

Day 11
Objective: Transition Tournament & 6v6
Warm-up: 2v2 Transition Tournament
Game: 6v6 Regular Scoring

---

Day 12
Objective: Continued Competitive Play
Warm-up: 2v2 Transition Tournament Continued
Game: 6v6 Regular Scoring (Game to 25)
