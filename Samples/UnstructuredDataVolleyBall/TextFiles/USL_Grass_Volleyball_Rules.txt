I. Facilities, Equipment, and Player Attire

Net Height:
Underdog uses a coed net height of 7’ 9 7/8” for league play.

Court Size:
Standard outdoor court: 16 x 8 m (52’6” x 26’3”).

Volleyballs:
Competition quality beach volleyballs provided by Underdog Sports Leagues.

Footwear:
Athletic shoes or barefoot recommended. Sandals or unsafe shoes not permitted.


---

II. League Format

Team Gender Ratios:
- No more than 2 men on court at one time.
- No fewer than 1 woman.
- Minimum 3 players required including at least 1 woman.
- 3 males and 1 female is NOT legal.

Officiating:
Self-officiated using honor system. Teams call their own violations.

Three Sets Count:
All 3 sets count toward standings (not best 2 of 3).

Scoring:
Rally scoring to 21, win by 2, no cap.

Match Duration:
Matches must be completed within 60 minutes. 
If third set unfinished, team ahead at hour mark wins (minimum 8 total points scored).


---

III. Rules Highlights

Gender Rules:
No reverse coed rules. Standard play.

Serves Touching Net:
Serves hitting net and going over are in play.

Service Attacks/Blocks:
Serve cannot be attacked or blocked above net height in front zone.

Setting the Serve:
Setting serve is allowed if clean (no carry/double).

Double Hit on Hard Attack:
Slight double on hard driven attack allowed if no carry.

Feet:
Foot saves allowed and count as a hit.

"Ball On":
Call only if ball enters your side of court and affects play.

Rotating:
Only server rotates on side out.

Touching the Net:
Any net contact during rally is a violation.

Playing Across Net:
Cannot follow ball past plane of net for safety.

Playoffs:
Seeding based on wins/ties. 
No non-roster subs unless pre-approved.
Players can play only one team per division in playoffs.


---

IV. Sportsmanship and Conduct

Supervisor has final authority on disputes and conduct.
Unsportsmanlike behavior will not be tolerated.
This is recreational volleyball – fun is the goal.
